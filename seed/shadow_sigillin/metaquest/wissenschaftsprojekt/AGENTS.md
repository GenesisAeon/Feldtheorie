# Metaquest Campaign Shadow Compass Protocol

This alcove records campaign-side Metaquest derailments and their
remedies.

- **Logistic guard.** $R$ = outreach or manuscript blockers,
  endorsement delays, simulator drift; $\Theta$ = parity brief +
  BreakPoint ritual echo + codex entry aligned within 24h;
  $\beta \approx 5.0$; $\zeta(R)$ flares when endorsements or rituals
  stall.
- **Tri-layer synchrony.** Update YAML/JSON/MD together and reference the
  light-side compass in
  `../../../bedeutungssigillin/metaquest/wissenschaftsprojekt/`.
- **Recovery first.** Detail triggers, diagnostics, and rituals (press
  kit freeze, manuscript addendum, outreach resync) for each alert.
- **Codex linkage.** Every change requires a codex entry carrying the
  mq-sci-shadow ids plus links into UTAC status and BreakPoint notes.
