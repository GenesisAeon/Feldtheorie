# Metaquest Campaign Shadow Lantern Shelf

> These shadow beacons keep watch over the campaign storyline. When endorsements,
> manuscripts, or telemetry fall out of resonance, they guide the recovery ritual so
> σ(β(R-Θ)) returns to the launch cadence.

## Logistic Guard
- **R** – Campaign risks: stalled endorsements, manuscript drift, silent telemetry.
- **Θ** – Parity brief + codex echo + bridge timestamp refreshed inside 24 h.
- **β ≈ 5.0** – Sharp response when gaps appear.
- **ζ(R)** – Flares if press-kit rehearsals or BreakPoint rituals lapse.

## Lanterns

### mq-sci-shadow-lantern-a — Manuscript Drift Alarm
- **Mirrors:** Light lantern `mq-sci-lantern-a`
- **Signals:** Parity brief missing latest manuscript update, codex lacks mq-sci tag.
- **Remedy:** Update parity brief + codex + UTAC matrix, timestamp campaign map and
  shadow compass with recovery note.

### mq-sci-shadow-lantern-b — Outreach Silence Alarm
- **Mirrors:** Light lantern `mq-sci-lantern-b`
- **Signals:** Bridge dashboard missing outreach stamp, codex silent on endorsements.
- **Remedy:** Rehearse press kit, archive transcript in codex, refresh bridge,
  escalate to `mq-sci-shadow-index-002` if cadence still misaligned.

### mq-sci-shadow-lantern-c — Telemetry Echo Gap
- **Mirrors:** Light lantern `mq-sci-lantern-c`
- **Signals:** No mq-sci codex entry within one sprint, bridge timestamp absent.
- **Remedy:** Run `scripts/sigillin_sync.py`, log run id, propagate to bridge and
  UTAC matrix, confirm shadow index closes.

## Recovery Hook
Every remediation adds a codex entry citing the shadow lantern id and references the
Metaquest section of `docs/utac_status_alignment_v1.2.md`. That keeps light and shadow
lanes braided.
